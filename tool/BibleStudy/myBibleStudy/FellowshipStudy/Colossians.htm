He is the image of the invisible God.

Gen 1:26. Then God said:"let us make man in our image, and in our likness,"
Image refer to the binary code in computer. Here it refers to character, quality--righteous, justic, integrity, mercy, grace, etc.
Likeness refers to appearance, quantity: say, see, hear, smell, walk, make, wisedom, love, bless,

"by HIM" solve the ontology issue, "where".
He is the origin of all.
"HE is the image" rather than "made in our image". HE is firstborn over all creation. He is before all thing. 
"the invisible God" is the Holy Spirit.

Metaphysics.:
"for HIM" solved the Metaphysic purpose issue: "why".
