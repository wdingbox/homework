Prophecy (Eph. 4:11; I Cor. 12:28)

SPEAKING
  1. Definition:
    1. Static View – the God-given ability to proclaim God’s truth in a timely and culturally sensitive fashion with a view toward edification, correction and repentance.
    2. Dynamic View – the God-given ability to speak human words to report something God brings to mind.
  2. Characteristics: quick to reference scriptures that are convicting, though not always comforting, ability to speak the truth in love.
