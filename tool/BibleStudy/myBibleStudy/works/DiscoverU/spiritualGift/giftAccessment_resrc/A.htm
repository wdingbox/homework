Administration (1 Cor. 12:28; Romans 12:8)

SERVING
  1. Definition: the God-given ability to organize and lead projects, to see them through from start to finish, while handling people tactfully and providing the vision to keep them at the task.
  2. Characteristics: pays close attention to details; able to supervise and manage others; good at multi-tasking; closely connected with characteristics of the gift of leadership.
