Evangelism (Eph. 4:11; Acts 21:8)

SPEAKING
  1. Definition: the God-given desire and ability to effectively communicate the message of the gospel of Christ to unbelievers.
  2. Characteristics: usually sees others come to Christ through their efforts, typically desires to spend time with non-Christians more often than Christians, can relate the gospel to others in a way that is understandable, ability to comfortably transition conversations to the topic of spiritual issues.
