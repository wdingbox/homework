Leadership (Eph. 4:11)

SERVING
  1. Definition: the God-given ability to stand confidently in a position of authority, and to attract, lead and motivate people to accomplish their work of ministry.
  2. Characteristics: visionary; often thinking big picture; love to mobilize volunteers, able to delegate; often find that people follow them; deeply passionate about a particular cause; closely connected with characteristics of the gift of administration.
