Conclusion

  1. From perspective of scientific argumentation, Gospel of John can be considered as a strict argumentative essay (议论文), that can be called argumentative Gospel. The seven claims and dual seven types of evidence discovered in Gospel of John are another features that can distinguish itself from synoptics.
  2. John 20:29 耶穌對他說：你因看見了我才信；那沒有看見就信的有福了。Then Jesus told him, “Because you have seen me, you have believed; blessed are those who have not seen and yet have believed.” Likewise, today, blessed are those believe in Jesus without knowing the proof through scientific argumentation process.
  3. Jesus is not about the truth that can be proved. The discovery of the strict argumentation process in Gosple of John is just another miraclious signs of Jesus. He is supernatual over any argumentation process. He do not need to be proved by anything. Scientific study is a tool for human beings not for Jesus. He is the truth himself. More than that, He is also the life and way. Most importantly He is the agape--the source of grace.
